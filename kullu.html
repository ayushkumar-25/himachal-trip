Go for River Rafting

Have fun indulging in breathtaking water sports in Kullu. The whitewater rafting packages proffered in Kullu are mind-blowing.

Rafting a 14 Km stretch in the mighty River, Beas. The journey begins from Piri and ends at Jhiri. Swimmers or nonswimmers just be carefree and hop into the boats and embark this thrilling journey.

Go for Paragliding

Spread your wings and fly like a bird above the enticing hills of the Himalayas. As you flutter in the blue sky admire the immaculate nature. The adventurous activity of Paragliding is one of the most enthralling things to do in Kullu.

Trip Advice

Should not stay at Kullu. Do sports and head towards Kasol which is 1 hr 6 min (38.8 km) from Kullu.

One night stay at Kasol (Camping).
